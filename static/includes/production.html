Large Scale Facility at Your Service

Production & Assembly @ 3rd Rail 3D Oakland, CA

3rd Rail will provide the most advanced 3D printing technology for production and assembly on the market. Stratasys and Makerbot will provide high and low-end 3D printers for low volume additive manufacturing and production.

  • Production quotes will be provided within 90 minutes of RFP with a minimum order of 50 units and shipping within 24 to 48 hours with premium pricing for orders requiring rush status
  • Tray sizes from 5"x 5"x 5" up to 36"x 24"x 36" available in FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) SLS (Selective Laser Sintering) in many materials and filaments including ABS and PLA and DMLS (Direct Metal Laser Sintering) in 5 different metals including aluminum and titanium
  • Customer reward scheduling available based on production run
  • Assembled products with up to 40 pieces will require a minimum production run of at least 50 units with shipping times of premium-priced 2 day, 1 week and 2 week turns
  • A variety of printing materials including ABS, PLA, and metals
  • 24 hour technical support
  • Customers can either drop-in to pick up or ship FedEx
