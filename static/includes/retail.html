3rd Rail 3D Retail Store in San Francisco

3rd Rail's retail location will provide customers with an immersive 3D printing experience unlike any store in San Francisco.

  • Customers will see a large assortment of 3D printers from a variety of manufacturers producing objects of art, jewelry, house wares, auto parts, and office items to name just a few.
  • Visitors can attend live demonstrations and workshops and buy cool gifts for friends and family.
  • Customers will be able to purchase 3D printers and supplies in-store or have them shipped.
  • Special manufacturer-direct discounts will be available to students attending colleges and universities in the US.
  • Free Wi-Fi will also be available along with good coffee priced at small mark-ups to cover costs.
  • The store will have a community area with wide screen monitors so customers may view tutorials and share ideas to drive entrepreneurship and innovation in 3D printing.
  • Event schedules will be available both online and onsite so our 3D audience can attend and innovate.
