What is 3D Printing?

3D printers are simpler versions of rapid prototyping machines that have been used by the automotive and aircraft industry for years. Although in recent years 3D printing and rapid prototyping have become synonymous together as is "additive manufacturing." Its benefits are lower costs, as little as $200 to buy a small fairly slow but capable printer, and increasing ease of use, up to machines in the $1,000 to $1,500 price range that offer incredibly intricate printing detail, relatively large builds, multiple colors, and faster print speeds.
