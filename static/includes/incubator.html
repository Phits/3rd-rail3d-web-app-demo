Incubator & Prototyping @ 3rd Rail 3D Retail Oakland, CA

Our prototyping and product incubation capabilities allow entrepreneurs and innovators to get their designs and ideas to market fast!

This space will be especially laid-out so customers can either get together in groups and/or seek the advice of our online/onsite experts to help design and build products. There will be community areas and shared-spaces complete with over-stuffed chairs and sofas with work tables so that 3rd Rail'ers will be comfortable for long hours collaborating and innovating. There will be free Wi-Fi and high speed internet with coffee & snack bar along video conferencing facilities available at a nominal charge. There will be a lounge area for taking breaks and playing games like ping-pong, video and board games.

Additionally, 3rd Rail will assist its customers in the following fashion.

  • 3rd Rail will provide demos, checklists, and tutorials to assist and ensure that entrepreneurs' designs are ready for 3D printing
  • Experts online and onsite will also be available to assist with 3D scanning and CAD software to support innovation so that creations can be realized and perfectly produced
  • Subscribers who've been certified by 3rd Rail will be permitted to access and operate our 3D equipment
  • File uploads to 3rd Rail will also be available so that designs can be printed by 3rd Rail immediately and shipped to customers within 24 hours
    • This will include on-the-spot pricing
    • A variety of printing materials including ABS, PLA, and metals
    • 24 hour technical support
  • Customer file uploads can be purchased at 3rd Rail's online marketplace
  • Customer feedback, rating, and chat provision will be available for all products and services helping drive the 3rd Rail experience
  • Invitations to monthly events that will include free beer and wine
